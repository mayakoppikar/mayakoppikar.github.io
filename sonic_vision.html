Sonic Vision: Head-Mounted Depth Perception Device

Sonic Vision is a wearable device designed to aid visually impaired users in perceiving depth information from their surroundings. Using ultrasonic sensors, the system measures distances to nearby objects and communicates feedback via vibration motors positioned on a headband.

Key Features

  • Ultrasonic sensors for accurate distance measurement
  • Haptic feedback through vibration motors for intuitive navigation
  • Lightweight, head-mounted design for portability
  • Microcontroller-based real-time signal processing
